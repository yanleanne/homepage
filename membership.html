Yan's Tennis Club allows members full use of courts and club facilities 7 days a week providing all an opportunity to join in the many organised in-house social competitions and social events organised by the club.

NOTE: When you first join the club a one-off pament will cover both the Joining Fee and Membership Fee. Membership fees are pro-rata depending on which month you join the club as a NEW member. In following years EXISTING members only pay the Yearly Annual Membership fee.
